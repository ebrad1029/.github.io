SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

With the recent rise in feedback shared on social media platforms,
companies have been collecting data to get a better understanding
on how their customers feel towards their company and their
products.
Sentiment Analysis is an automated process where machine learning techniques are applied to identify or extract the subjective information of a text.
It is used to provide companies with interesting insights about their customers’ feedback and
therefore, sentiment analysis is proven essential for any company that wants to know how their company is doing.

OUR PROJECT

In this project, we want to grasp the overall perception of State Farm’s customers through sentiment analysis among the social media platforms that State Farm is associated with.
We did this by collecting customers’ tweets that were posted during critical State Farm events (i.e. The Superbowl)
and then, with that data, we found out how customers’ felt towards State Farm.
This data was put into graphs created in R and Python and then presented to State Farm in the form of a website.

Our final analysis includes the peaks and valleys in sentiment during critical events in the company’s history.
We created this website to present our findings to State Farm.
This data will not only help State Farm, but it will help their customers as well, as State Farm will be able to use these results
to help better their customers either through providing better options for their products or by providing better customer service.
